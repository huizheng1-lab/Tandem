Autonomy policy:
- `Add -Epic -Autonomy full` records per-item full autonomy; omitted autonomy follows the human-editable `AutonomyDefault: plan-gated|autonomous` board line.
- The shipped board default is `plan-gated`.
- Autonomous epics retain the plan-only first turn and independent peer validation.
- After validation, `AutoApprovePlan -Commit <accepted-stable>` verifies the relay stable ref, records `approval=auto`, and appends `wishlist.planAutoApprove` to the control-panel audit log.
- A noted Requeue during an autonomous plan or step is a retroactive rejection that preserves completed history, increments the plan revision, and returns the epic to planning.
- Authentication, credentials, pairing, and remote-control items are forced to plan-gated mode. Runtime promotion, master integration, and every protocol safety boundary remain human-only.
